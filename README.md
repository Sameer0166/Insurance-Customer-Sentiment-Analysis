# Insurance Analysis & Customer Sentiment Dashboard

## Project Overview

The **Insurance Analysis & Customer Sentiment Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI solution developed to analyze insurance business performance alongside customer feedback. The project integrates structured insurance data with sentiment analysis to evaluate customer satisfaction, identify service improvement opportunities, and support better business decisions.

By combining operational metrics with customer opinions, this dashboard provides a comprehensive view of both business performance and customer experience.

---

## Business Problem

Insurance companies receive large volumes of customer feedback every day. While operational metrics measure business performance, understanding customer satisfaction requires analyzing textual feedback.

This project was developed to answer questions such as:

- How satisfied are customers with insurance services?
- What percentage of customers provide positive or negative feedback?
- Which service areas require improvement?
- How can customer sentiment support business decision-making?

## Data Preparation

The project involved multiple data preparation steps before visualization:

- Imported insurance operational data from SQL Server.
- Imported customer feedback from Excel.
- Processed customer reviews using Python.
- Generated sentiment scores.
- Classified customer feedback into sentiment categories.
- Created relationships between datasets.
- Developed DAX measures for dashboard reporting.
